Team Canada vs COVID-19

Puck off to the Pandemic

This site calculates various averages of increase (and hopefully decrease) in cases of CoVid-19 in Canada. The sooner we get rid of this, the sooner we get hockey and other stuff back!


If you are wondering why the day-to-day case numbers are not the same as data from some other sites you are seeing; different sites collect data at different daily cut off times. The important thing to keep in mind is, in the long run, the numbers are more or less the same across different sites reporting on CoVid-19.


We focus on the trend over time, using a statistical concept called rolling average.
