Puck off CoVid-19!

This site calculates various averages of increase (and hopefully decrease) in cases of CoVid-19 in Canada. The sooner we get rid of this, the sooner we get hockey and other stuff back!


If you are wondering why the case numbers are not the same as data from some other sites you are seeing; different sites collect data at different cut off times. The important thing is in the long run, the numbers are more or less the same across different sites reporting on CoVid-19.


We focus on the trend over time, using a statistical concept called rolling average.
